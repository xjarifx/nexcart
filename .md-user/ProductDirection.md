# Nexcart

**A flexible e-commerce platform that scales from a single storefront to a full multi-vendor marketplace.**

---

## What It Is

Nexcart is a production-ready platform built to support the full spectrum of e-commerce — from an independent seller launching their first store, to a marketplace hosting thousands of vendors.

## What It Includes

**Storefront & Discovery** — A polished landing page and browsable product/shop discovery experience for end users.

**Seller Tools** — A dedicated panel for vendors to manage their inventory, orders, and store appearance.

**Admin Controls** — Platform-level oversight covering users, sellers, and marketplace activity.

**Flexible Theming** — Multiple UI themes built in, so each store can reflect its own identity.

**Reusable Architecture** — The codebase is modular by design — easy to redeploy, extend, or white-label for new projects.

---

## How It Scales

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| Solo Seller | One vendor, full control over branding and storefront |
| Multi-Vendor | Open platform where multiple sellers list and sell independently |
